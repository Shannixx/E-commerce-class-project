About Elegant Wear

Redefining luxury formal fashion since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Elegant Wear began with a simple vision: to create formal wear that combines timeless elegance with modern sophistication. Our journey started in a small boutique and has grown into a global brand trusted by professionals worldwide.

We believe that exceptional formal wear should be accessible to everyone who values quality and craftsmanship. Each piece in our collection is carefully designed and crafted using premium materials, ensuring that you always look and feel your best.

Today, Elegant Wear stands as a symbol of refined style, serving thousands of customers who appreciate the perfect blend of tradition and innovation in formal fashion.

Returns & Exchanges

Our hassle-free return policy ensures your complete satisfaction

Return Policy
  • 30-Day Returns
    From the delivery date for a full refund.
  • Original Condition
    Items must be unworn, unwashed, with tags.
  • Full Refund
    Processed to original payment method (5-7 days).
How to Return
  1. Contact customer service to initiate.
  2. Pack items securely in original packaging.
  3. Attach the prepaid return label (emailed).
  4. Drop off at an authorized shipping location.
  5. Track your return online.
Exchanges

Need a different size/color? Follow return process & place a new order. We offer free exchanges.

Non-Returnable Items
  • Altered or tailored items
  • Final sale items
  • Items without original tags
